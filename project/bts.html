About

BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan), also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band that was formed in 2010 and debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment. Consisting of members Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. They co-write and co-produce much of their own output. Originally a hip hop group, their musical style has evolved to include a wide range of genres. Their lyrics, often focused on personal and social commentary, touch on the themes of mental health, troubles of school-age youth and coming of age, loss, the journey towards loving oneself, and individualism. Their work also often references literature and psychological concepts and includes an alternative universe storyline.

Signature notables

BTS has broken many records and recieved over 400 awards. Although when it comes to the most notable achievement, It would be this. The members of BTS were involved with several philanthropic organizations. In 2017 they teamed with UNICEF to launch the Love Myself campaign, which raised funds to support social programs fighting violence against children and teens.

In 2021 South Korean Pres. Moon Jae-In appointed the members of BTS as special presidential envoys for future generations and culture. As such, the band members took turns giving a speech ahead of the annual United Nations General Assembly meeting of world leaders in New York, New York, and showed a video of themselves performing their latest single, “Permission to Dance,” which had been shot in the UN General Assembly Building’s iconic Assembly Hall and on the grounds nearby. BTS encouraged the world’s youth to become a beacon of change amid the coronavirus SARS-CoV-2 pandemic and urged people to get vaccinated. This according to me in the biggest and the proudest achievement for BTS

Author Favorites

No More Dream


The song is the lead single that BTS debuted with in 2013. It was received with strong impressions within the k-pop industry, for it’s honest and motivational lyrics, and it’s authenticity (compared to other k-pop songs at the time).BTS are trying to encourage them to stand up and have their own dreams and pursue those dreams and not focus so much on these things and be forced into someone else’s dream.

Blood Sweat & Tears


On the single “Blood Sweat & Tears” from the group’s 2016 album, Wings, tropical house blends with moombahton production as members Jimin, Jungkook, Suga, RM, J-Hope, V, and Jin declare their willingness to throw everything on the line for love, success or whatever their aim might be. Thematically, the guys blend sentiments of temptation with an often carnal willingness to sacrifice everything.

Butter


“Butter” is a dance-pop song with lyrical confessions of sweet, romantic love. The fun and witty lyrics brimming with positive and bright energy will instantly uplift moods. The track uniquely shows each of the BTS' member’s charms as they show how smooth and at times charismatic, they can be. The song is dubbed as a summer anthem credited to its distinctive bass line and crisp synth sounds, ready to melt the listener’s heart.

It becomes their second English song following “Dynamite” and will receive a more widespread promotion on radio stations. It is also their 3rd track to debut at #1 on the Hot 100, and their 4th track to hit #1 on the Hot 100.

Magic Shop


This song, is my personal favorite. This has always been the song that I listen to when I need to get out of my head for a bit; it helps me feel so much better. It always assures me with the safety that my heart and mind require from time to time.

With the help of BTS' youngest member Jungkook for the production of the song, “Magic Shop” is a track that surrounds a concept about how if fans look inside their hearts, they will find a magic shop where they can exchange their fears for happiness and find the love all of the members have for them.

During the BTS press conference on May 24, 2018, Jungkook described the track as a song that could be comforting for ARMYs—whenever they felt tired, sad, or frustrated they could just have to open the door in their heart to the magic shop and there they would find the seven members of BTS waiting for them.

About

Shawn Peter Raul Mendes (born August 8, 1998) is a Canadian singer and songwriter. He gained a following in 2013, posting song covers on the video-sharing application "Vine". He also became the third most followed musician on "vine" in the year 2015. Young talent has always been on the rise and youngsters have been very well received by the US market. Shawn Mendes is one such young blood who has done very well at a young age by appealing to the mass population. To our surprise, he even auditioned for the Disney Channel in Toronto to resume his acting career.

Blog

Shawn has seen great success as a musical artist from a young age. The singer-songwriter became one of the first artists ever (of five total) to debut at number one before turning 18 years old. He was the third-youngest artist to have three number-one singles on the Billboard Adult Contemporary chart before he turned 20 years old. For me, Shawn Mendes is a guy who showered me with a whole lot of pleasing positive vibes. Recently some of his songs truly mould me as a character because they helped me with my overthinking and social anxieties. He even disclosed that he was going through therapy to help with his anxiety in an interview still he came out of it using his songs. I could perfectly able to connect to him as a person through his song lyrics. Apart from his musical talent he also has a generous characteristic perhaps we can say a “philanthropist”. He has donated time and money to DoSomething.org, Pencils of Promise, the American Red Cross, the Mercury Phoenix Trust, and more. I’m very grateful that I found his songs!! These are true lifesavers for me.

Author's Favorites

Senorita


Shawn Mendes, Camila Cabello new song Senorita sets internet ABLAZZEE!!. The track senorita has struck the right chord with their fans as it depicts a sizzling chemistry between the two. The steamy and saucy video of the song does complete justice to the track that talks about friends with benefits who are ready to take the plunge and want to make things more official. My favorite verse is : "I love it when you call me senorita I wish I could pretend I didn't need ya :)"

In My Blood


OVERTHINKING AND ANXIETY REALLY SUCKS! Shawn battles his anxiety and loneliness through his emotional song, "In My Blood." It marks the first time the singer has publicly discussed his experiences with the mental health disorder.(must be really hard for him :\ ) Whenever I felt low and insecure I listen to this song as it always made me feel secure once again and also it made sure that i shouldn't give up no matter what! My favorite verse is : "Laying on the bathroom floor, feeling nothing I'm overwhelmed and insecure, give me something I could take to ease my mind slowly"

Its really really hard to see the love of your life hanging around with someone else. And what makes it even more worse is that they wont be anymore getting the same love and care you gave instead they will be mistreated and taken for granted :( .If you already have a past and listen to this then you may even cry hard till your emotions held staright again.The lyrics are just soo relatable. My favorite verse is : "Cause I know I can treat you better than he can And any girl like you deserves a gentleman Tell me why are we wasting time On all your wasted crying When you should be with me instead?"

It'll Be Okay
